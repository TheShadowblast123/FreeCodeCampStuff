What is a pantoum?

A pantoum is a poetic form with repetitive quatrains orginating in Malaysia and is known for its rhythmic and reflective qualities. The even lines of each stanza become the odd lines of the next, until the very end where the first odd lines repeat.

That sounds cool! But isn't it hard?

The repeating nature of the poem is difficult to tame, but very beautiful when executed well, and that's the point of this drafter! Simply start drafting your poem and the lines will show up where they're supposed to go, so you only have to think of the poem as a whole!
