What is a Villanelle?

A Villanelle is a 19-line poetic form known for its repetitive structure and rhyme scheme. It consists of five tercets and a quatrain, with specific lines repeated throughout the poem. The repetition creates a distinct rhythm and highlights key themes or ideas.

So how does this help?

You write the first tercet rhyming the first and third lines and the drafter will fill out a format. This format will contain the necessary rhyme scheme and fill out the repeated lines required to continue the poem. You can decide to either download the format or copy it to continue working in another editor.
